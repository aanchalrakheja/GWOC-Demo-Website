Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is it strictly for girls or can boys also participate?

    GirlScript seeks to help anyone who is new to the field of development, and it promotes an inclusive environment in particular. As a result, GWoC is open to both girls and boys. Everyone is welcome to participate and contribute to the growth of the Content Repository and our effort.

  • How will participants communicate with mentors and admins?

    All project-related communication will take place in the project's Discord/Slack channel.

  • How will I know that I am selected?

    Participants will be notified via email and will receive an invitation to the Discord/Slack workspace.

  • What is the criterion for selection as a participant?

    Your devotion and desire to contribute to the Content Repository are the only criteria for selection.

  • When are the registrations beginning?

    1st August’21

  • What is the registration deadline?

    25th August’21

  • Which countries and states are eligible to participate in the program?

    People from every country and state are welcome to participate in this program.
